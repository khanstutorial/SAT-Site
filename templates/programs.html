59 Topics in Our Curriculum

We divide our SAT curriculum into 12 reading topics, 25 math topics, and 22 grammar topics. In each class, students learn at least 5 new topics and practice at least 40 questions per topic. Each topic offers extensive classwork, homework, and review questions that are similar to the questions on the actual College Board SAT.

New Strategies and Techniques

Our SAT curriculum covers new strategies and techniques that no other review book or instructor will mention! These strategies can be used to help you answer questions in the snap of a finger and get them correct. Our customized review sheets will help you remember the information quickly and easily. All our questions and review sheets are unique and handmade, so you can't get them anywhere else.

4,000+ Practice Questions in 8 Weeks

Each topic consists of 20 questions for classwork, 20 for homework, and 20 for review. At the end of each week, students are assigned review problem sets in which they practice additional questions in ALL the topics they learned that week.

6 Diagnostic Tests and Review

We administer 6 handmade diagnostic tests that closely reflects the actual College Board SAT. Following each test, we review all questions and go over strategies to finish quicker and get an even score.

Daily Quizzes

Each class starts off with a small quiz that is based on the last class's topics, followed by review. This ensures that whatever you're learning stays in your head, from now until test day.
